AIRDROP BASICS

What is an Airdrop?

👤 BSCBRC • 📖 5 min read • 📅 Updated July 2026

A crypto airdrop is a marketing strategy where blockchain projects distribute free tokens to users. The goal is to increase awareness, reward early supporters, and encourage community growth.

Users may qualify by completing simple tasks, using a protocol, holding specific tokens, or actively participating in a project's ecosystem before the official token launch.

WHY AIRDROPS

Why Projects Launch Airdrops

Projects use airdrops to attract new users, reward early adopters, and build a strong community before launching their token.

Airdrops also encourage users to explore the ecosystem, provide feedback, and increase onchain activity without traditional advertising.

Types of Airdrops

Types of Airdrops

There are several types of crypto airdrops, each with different eligibility requirements and goals.

  • Standard Airdrop: Free tokens for eligible wallet addresses.
  • Holder Airdrop: Rewards users who hold a specific cryptocurrency.
  • Testnet Airdrop: Rewards early users who test a blockchain before launch.
  • Retroactive Airdrop: Rewards users based on their past onchain activity.
STAY SAFE

How to Stay Safe

Always verify official project announcements before connecting your wallet or signing transactions.

  • Never share your Secret Recovery Phrase.
  • Use a separate wallet for airdrop farming.
  • Bookmark official project websites.
  • Ignore fake direct messages and phishing links.
COMMON MISTAKES

Common Mistakes

Many users miss valuable airdrops because of simple mistakes.

  • Using fake websites.
  • Ignoring wallet security.
  • Expecting every project to launch a token.
  • Creating multiple wallets against project rules.
  • Following hype instead of doing research.
FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are crypto airdrops really free?
Most legitimate airdrops are free, but you may need to pay small blockchain gas fees.

Can I receive multiple airdrops?
Yes, if you meet the eligibility requirements of different projects.

How can I avoid airdrop scams?
Always verify official links, never share your recovery phrase, and research every project before participating.
