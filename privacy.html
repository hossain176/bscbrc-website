PRIVACY

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us.

This Privacy Policy explains what information BSCBRC collects, how it is used, and how we protect your data while you use our website.

OVERVIEW

Our Privacy Commitment

BSCBRC respects your privacy and only collects the minimum information necessary to improve our educational content and website experience.

We do not sell personal information and we never ask for wallet private keys or Secret Recovery Phrases.

DATA

Information We Collect

We may collect basic website analytics, browser information and device details to improve your experience.

If you contact us through X, Telegram or GitHub, we only receive the information you choose to share.

We never collect your wallet private keys or Secret Recovery Phrase.

USAGE

How We Use Information

Information is used only to improve our website, create better educational content and respond to community questions.

We also use anonymous analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website.

THIRD-PARTY

Third-Party Services

BSCBRC may link to external platforms such as X, Telegram and GitHub.

These websites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content or practices.

YOUR RIGHTS

Your Rights

You may choose not to share personal information with us at any time.

If you contact us and later wish your shared information to be removed, you may request its deletion.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is BSCBRC free to use?
Yes. All educational content on BSCBRC is freely available.

Does BSCBRC collect wallet data?
No. We never collect wallet private keys or Secret Recovery Phrases.

Can this Privacy Policy change?
Yes. We may update this Privacy Policy whenever necessary.
